Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Information We Collect

The Antigravity Attention Engine collects and processes:

  • X API Data: Your home timeline tweets (for content inspiration) and your posted tweets (for performance measurement).
  • API Credentials: Your X API keys, stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify trending topics and generate content suggestions.
  • To post tweets and replies on your behalf (with your authorization).
  • To measure engagement on your posts and improve suggestions.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers. API credentials are stored in a local .env file.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. The only external communication is with X's API and Google's Gemini API (for content generation).

5. Data Retention

Data is retained locally until you delete it. You can clear all stored data by removing the data/ directory.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can delete the application and all associated data at any time.

7. Contact

For questions about this policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
